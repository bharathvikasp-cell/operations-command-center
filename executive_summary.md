# Executive Summary

## Data Scope
- 10,431 service cases
- 5 teams
- 3 months of activity

## Key Findings
1. Overall SLA met rate was **69.2%**.
2. The issue category contributing the highest volume of SLA misses was **Technical** with **1,117 missed cases**.
3. Overall reopen rate was **6.9%**. **Tech Support** had the highest reopen rate at **13.5%**.
4. There were **1,134 open cases** at period end, and **1,048** were older than 7 days.
5. The **Morning** shift carried the highest open backlog.
6. The most common root-cause bucket was **Manual Review Delay** with **2,542 cases**.
7. Escalation rate was **9.0%** and average CSAT was **4.27 / 5**.

## Recommended Actions
- Rebalance staffing on the **Morning** shift.
- Create a targeted SOP / knowledge refresh for **Tech Support**.
- Review **Technical** for automation or routing changes.
- Address the leading root cause (**Manual Review Delay**) with a focused improvement plan.
