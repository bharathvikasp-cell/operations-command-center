# Portfolio Bullets

- Built an Operations Command Center project in SQL and Power BI to analyze backlog aging, SLA performance, escalation trends, reopen rates, and customer satisfaction across 10,000+ service cases.
- Identified top drivers of SLA misses and repeat workload through root-cause and reopen-rate analysis, producing action-oriented recommendations for staffing and process improvement.
